A Note from the Author

The poets who preserved Bellerophon’s story remembered the Chimera, the golden bridle, the moment of flight. They did not linger on the Aleian Plain — the years of walking, the slow erasure, the distance between what a man was and what he became. This book tried to remember the plain as well as the sky.

If ‘The Fall from Heaven’ found its way to you, it has made it some distance off the plain. A review on Amazon or Goodreads is how books travel. The story of Bellerophon nearly vanished entirely; a few lines in Homer were all that survived. A review is a kind of keeping.
